# Changelog

## 0.1.0 | 16 May 2026

- Adds the pallet to the standard wooden build desk and the cellar build desk, costing 6 flitch and 4 nails. Vanilla only sells pallets via the barn's storage desk for 6 flitch and 1 marketing point (a questline currency you can't farm), so they're effectively locked behind the merchant questline. This entry lets you build them from the start.
- Pallets can be removed via the build desk's Remove button, returning half the build cost.
- The cellar elevator now fills pallets across the whole cellar room, not just the small invisible zone right next to the elevator. The porter that ships crates from the cellar to the barn also sweeps the whole cellar room. Build pallets anywhere in the cellar and the system picks them up.
- Pallets placed in the barn add merchant trading capacity. Pallets dropped anywhere else just work as extra single-slot containers.
